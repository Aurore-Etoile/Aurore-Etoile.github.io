英语口语笔记 1

Author: Aurore  Date: February 5, 2020  22:10:39   Category: 英语学习笔记

真人秀

hitshows 热播剧
rearity show 真人秀
Rearity show is a big hit! 真人秀很红！
a big fan of 是…的粉丝
I’m a big fan of this hitshow!
fanboy/fangirl 脑残粉
talent show 选秀
I’m not that big on it! 我对这个无感！
TV series 电视剧
It’s really not to my taste. 这真不是我的菜。

看电影

Would you like to see a film?
Which film would you like to see?
Which showtime would you like to choose? 你想看哪个场次？
Which seat would you like to take? 你想选哪个位子？
Would you like some popcorn?

下午茶

afternoon tea
low tea
What are you in the mood for? 你想吃什么？
I’m in the mood for… 我想吃……
This scone doesn’t agree with me. 我不爱吃司康饼。

西餐

appetizer 前菜
soup 汤
entree 主菜
dessert 甜点
drink 饮料
Excuse me, we’re ready to order.
I’ll have this, this…
How do you like your steak? 牛排你要几分熟？
Medium.
Medium well.
Well done.
Check please. 买单。
It’s on me./It’s my treat. 我请客。
Let’s go dutch!

砍价

How much do you want for this?
Come on. That’s steep, isn’t it?
I can get this much cheaper at other places!
What’s the lowest price that you can go?
It’s still out of my price range.

扫货季

It’s such a rip-off! 抢钱啊！
Are you ripping me off? 你宰我吧？
good deal 便宜货
bargain hunt 淘便宜货
flash sale 限时折扣
doorbuster sale 限量限低价抢购
clearance sale 清仓大甩卖
discount 折扣

放鸽子

stand sb up
I’m terribly sorry for…
It’s all my fault.
Please give me a chance, I’ll make it up to you.

约会

Let’s grab a drink or a bite this week?
What’s your schedule like?
… is good for me/works fine.
Let’s say … maybe?

夸赞

small talk 闲聊
You look so gorgeous tonight.
You can say that again. 你说得对。
You said it.
There you go.
You took the words right out of my mouth.

表白

I have a crush on you.
You had me from hello.
You are the one for me.
We’re made for each other.
I’m head over heels for you.
